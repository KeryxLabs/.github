# **KeryxLabs**  

Tech lost something.  

Not to AI,before AI. 
The internet used to be a place where people showed up as themselves.
Tools had personality. Problems required creativity.
Participation meant something because it cost something.

We traded that for ease and speed.
Everything got smoother and blander at the same time.
Even the tools we use to build things stopped feeling like ours.

KeryxLabs exists to build differently.  
Not faster. Not easier. **More meaningful.**

We create tools that **augment how you think instead of replacing your thinking**.
Infrastructure that makes data **legible and actionable**, not outsourced.
Software that has a **point of view**, because the people who built it had one.

We are not an AI lab.
We are a **modern data integration and reasoning lab**. 
AI is a lens. Data is the substance. Humans are still the point.

We believe computation, whether over code, knowledge, or thought can be **structured, observable, and interpretable**.
That’s why we built ACC and STTP:

- ACC models **codebases as living systems**, quantifying stability, logic, friction, and autonomy, and providing **actionable insight** directly in your editor.
    
- STTP (Spatio-Temporal Transfer Protocol) applies the same principles to **reasoning and conversation**, letting ideas, knowledge, and thought be **tracked, simulated, and understood**.
    

Together, they demonstrate a new paradigm: AI is not an omniscient oracle.
It is a **stateless machine** operating on biases and structure provided by its creators, computing knowledge into a digital format humans can **observe, interact with, and improve**.

Herald. Memory. Mind. Understanding.
